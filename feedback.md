## 4. About Empower Mind Initiative

### About Empower Mind Initiative

Empower Mind Initiative was founded in November 2022 as a youth-focused organization dedicated to promoting mental health, psychosocial well-being, and social resilience among adolescents, young people, women, and other vulnerable populations.

Through community empowerment, youth engagement, advocacy, research, and systems strengthening, the organization responds to the growing burden of mental health challenges in Malawi and beyond. These challenges include depression, anxiety, suicide risk, substance use, trauma, and psychosocial pressures associated with education, unemployment, climate shocks, and social inequality.

Since its founding, Empower Mind Initiative has reached more than 5,000 adolescents and young people across Zomba and Machinga districts. The organization works through a growing network of Mental Health Ambassadors operating within five university chapters and secondary schools.

Empower Mind Initiative has also collaborated with local and international organizations to expand access to mental health psychoeducation, strengthen youth-led advocacy, and build sustainable community resilience.

## 8. Typography Improvements

Replace the current heading typography if it feels generic, outdated, inconsistent, or difficult to read.

Use a modern and professional font combination.

Recommended direction:

- Headings: **Manrope**, **Plus Jakarta Sans**, **Sora**, or **DM Sans**
- Body text: **Inter**, **Manrope**, or **DM Sans**

Use no more than two font families across the website.

Recommended hierarchy:

- Main hero heading: 56–72px on large screens
- Main hero heading on mobile: 38–46px
- Section headings: 38–48px on large screens
- Section headings on mobile: 28–34px
- Card headings: 20–26px
- Body text: 16–18px
- Small labels: 13–15px

Typography requirements:

- Maintain consistent heading sizes across similar sections.
- Use comfortable line heights.
- Avoid excessively bold body text.
- Keep paragraph widths between approximately 55 and 75 characters.
- Ensure headings wrap naturally on smaller screens.
- Avoid using uppercase for long headings.
- Ensure sufficient text contrast.

- Use clean content grids.

- Use strong photography featuring authentic youth and community engagement.
- Use calm and optimistic colors.

- Use one main accent color and one supporting accent color.

- A restrained warm accent for important highlights
