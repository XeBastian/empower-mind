# Quality gates

A page is not complete until it passes all applicable gates.

## Content

- No invented facts.
- No unexplained placeholder copy in production.
- Claims have a source or approval status.
- Dates and program status are visible where useful.
- Calls to action lead somewhere real.
- Mental-health wording passes dignity and safeguarding review.

## Visual

- The page has a clear focal point.
- Section rhythm varies intentionally.
- Typography works at 320px and wide desktop widths.
- No accidental template patterns.
- Photos are purposeful and correctly cropped.
- The design remains coherent when animation is disabled.
- Empty, loading, error, and long-content states are designed.

## Interaction

- Keyboard navigation works.
- Focus is visible and logical.
- Mobile navigation is usable.
- Forms preserve input and explain errors.
- Reduced-motion behavior is verified.
- Links and buttons are semantically correct.

## Engineering

- Typecheck passes.
- Lint passes.
- Automated tests pass.
- Production build passes.
- No console errors.
- No unexpected horizontal overflow.
- Images have dimensions and responsive sources.
- Metadata, sitemap, robots, social cards, and canonical URLs are intentional.

## Responsive review widths

At minimum inspect:

- 320px
- 375px
- 768px
- 1024px
- 1440px

Also inspect content stress cases rather than only ideal mock content.
