# Empower Mind creative direction

Status: implementation note, 2026-07-12

## Direction

Build an expressive editorial system around participation, movement, and institutional credibility. Deep navy provides authority; amber, coral, lilac, and sky create controlled colour chapters. Large compact type, offset rules, cropped circles, and staggered fields create energy without relying on photography or decorative illustration.

The reference's useful principles are translated as bold colour blocking, asymmetrical pacing, strong type hierarchy, layered section edges, and varied programme compositions. Its branding, copy, assets, and layouts are not reproduced.

## Content rules

- `project-content/empower-mind-profile.json` is the current authoritative working source.
- Programme status, locations, contacts, institutions, and accountability claims must trace to that file.
- The January–December 2026 activity phases are published as a plan, not evidence of completion.
- The palette is provisional until the referenced logo asset is added.
- No photography appears until approved documentary assets and rights records are supplied.
- The site remains informational and does not present itself as an emergency service.

## Experience

The homepage moves from a clear Malawi-specific proposition into context, a connected response model, the chapter network, distinct programme previews, accountability, and a direct partnership contact. Each section uses a different composition while sharing grid, type, colour, focus, and motion rules.
